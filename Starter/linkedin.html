How to Use Linkedin Like a Pro

October 19, 2024 3 Min Read

1. Choose a Professional Profile Picture

Include a recent headshot that clearly shows your face so people you connect with can easily recognize you.

2. Add a Background Picture

Select a background banner that showcases more about your or your role. This can be something related to yoru company, university, or field of profession.

3. Include a Short Linkedin Headline

Use this to immediately state your role, what kind of work you're interested in, quickly mention your most notable professional accomplishments, or mention your professional goals or mission.

4. Include More Information in the About Section

Use this section to tell yoru story and share who you are, what you're currently doing, what you're interested in, or what you're interested in exploring next.

5. List Your Work Experiences

This section might come much easier if you already have a resume. List your experiences including who you worked with, how long, and your contribition. It is helpful to include statistics when you can and short, readible descriptions.

6. Fill Out Your Skills

Make sure to highlight and include relevant skills that are related and impressive for your current line of work.

7. Connect With People and Grow Your Network!

Don't be afraid to share content, comments, and make connection requests to actively grow your network.
