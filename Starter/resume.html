2. Contact Information

Make sure to add your professional email, full name, and phone number somewhere on your resume.

3. Add Your Resume Headline or Summary

This is a quick summary with two to three sentences about your job title, experience, relevant skills, and accomplishments that should be near the top of your resume right below your name and contact information.

4. Include Your Work Experience

Make sure you have a section on your resume for your work experiences and include the company name, location, description, start and end dates, and a bulleted summary of achievments and responsibilities (include statistics and numbers whenever you can).

5. Include Your Education

Make sure you include the program name, university name, dates attended, honors/awards, achievements, GPA, etc.

6. List Both Your Hard and Soft Skills

Hard skills are measurable abilities (ex: coding languages) and soft skills are personal traits or qualities (ex: leadership).

7. Other Sections

Feel free to include any other of these sections:

Languages, hobbies/interests, certifications, publications, awards, volunteer experience, personal projects, and extracurricular activities.
