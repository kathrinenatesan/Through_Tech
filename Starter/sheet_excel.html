Using Google Sheets and Excel

October 19, 2024 2 Min Read

So what’s the difference between the two? Here’s a simple Venn diagram to break down their similarities and differences.

Components of Google Sheets

Cell: A single element or data point.

Column: Vertical set of cells.

Row: Horizontal set of cells.

Range: Selection of cells extending across a row, column, or both.

Function: Built-in operation from spreadsheet app.

Formula: Combination of functions, cells, rows, columns, and ranges to obtain a specific result.
